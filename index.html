About Our School

A new academy, built for the long term

Every classroom, every column, every gate on our campus was built with one generation in mind: yours.

Global Hilton International Academy sits at #15 Ikot Akpakang Road, Ibiaku Obio Ndobo, off Airport Road in Uruan LGA. Our campus was purpose-built — not converted from another use — with proper classroom blocks, a perimeter wall for safety, and space for children to learn and play without crowding.

We run a full pathway from Early Childhood Education (Creche & Nursery) through Primary, Junior Secondary and Senior Secondary School, so a child can grow with us from their very first day of school to graduation.
